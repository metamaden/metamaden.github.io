About

Sean Maden, Ph.D., is a bioinformatics researcher with expertise in transcriptomics, epigenomics, and informatics analysis. He is interested in open-access research projects for data science.

Sean has developed dashboards, workflows, pipelines, applets, websites, and software packages. His open-source development experience includes 3 packages contributed to the Bioconductor repository (lute, cellScaleFactors, and recountmethylation).
