## Software

* `2020` [recountmethylation](https://www.bioconductor.org/packages/devel/bioc/html/recountmethylation.html), Bioconductor/R package

Utilities to obtain, query, and analyze cross-study compilations of DNAm array datasets from the Gene Expression Omnibus. Includes vignettes providing background about data types, reproductions of published analyses, and implementations of published software to perform power analyses, predict genetic ancestry from DNA methylation array data, and more.

* `2021` [recountmethylation_instance](https://github.com/metamaden/recountmethylation_instance), Snakemake workflow

Synchronize public DNAm array data. This resource provides a full Snakemake workflow to download, harmonize, and compile IDAT-derived DNAm and SOFT-derived sample metadata from GEO experiments.
