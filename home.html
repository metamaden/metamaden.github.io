Bio



Sean grew up in and around the Pacific Northwest. He is a fun-loving vegetarian who is happily married to his life companion, Lindsay. They live in King City near the Portland Metro area of Oregon state with their cat Suki. When he is not immersed in data science or programming projects, Sean loves to read, cook, walk, hike, run, listen to music, and watch shows.










Professional experience:



Data Analyst | Bioinformatics Researcher | Transcriptomics | Epigenomics | Informatics Analysis

Sean Maden obtained his Ph.D. Computational Biology/Biomedical Engineering in 2022. He is a senior analyst and bioinformatics researcher specializing in transcriptomics, epigenomics, and informatics analysis. With a passion for open-access research projects in data science, Sean combines scientific rigor with innovative solutions.

Key Highlights:

Transcriptomics Expertise: Sean’s deep understanding of transcriptomics allows him to unravel complex gene expression patterns and identify critical regulatory elements.

Epigenomics Insights: Sean’s work in epigenomics sheds light on how modifications to DNA impact gene function, providing valuable insights for personalized medicine and disease research.

Informatics Analysis: Sean excels in analyzing large-scale biological datasets, extracting meaningful information, and translating it into actionable knowledge.

Open-Source Contributions: Sean’s commitment to the scientific community is evident through his contributions to the Bioconductor repository. Notably, he has developed three open-source packages: lute, cellScaleFactors, and recountmethylation.

Software Development: Sean’s skill set extends beyond analysis. He has designed interactive dashboards, streamlined workflows, robust pipelines, user-friendly applets, and responsive websites.

Data Science: Sean has over 8 years of experience applying a complement of analysis, statistics, reporting, and programming skills to carry out data science for research.
